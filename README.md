# Adventure-Quest
Interactive JavaScript Console-Based Game

# Short Description
In each room, a scenario description appears, and the player must pick choices between A and B, and also Ok or Cancel for confirmations. Bad choices deduct 1 heart point from their starting pool of 3 hearts. Bad choices can still continue and sometimes can lead to rewards as long as you still have hearts


# 🎮 Game Design

## Game Objectives
- Navigate through all 5 story chapters without the player's health reaching 0 or lower.
- Strategically gain weapon, strength and agility stats to pass specific conditions to avoid losing health or losing the game.
- Reach the final boss room and win the battle using weapons, gained stats or by the help of your allies.

## Score/ Reward System
Going through scenarios, with your choices you can gain rewards, but some choices you might miss getting the rewards. Through these rewards, your chances of winning against the boss increases.
- Gain Stats
- Gain a Weapon
- Help allies
